About Long Wave

Long Wave is a digital magazine created to encourage and showcase female surfers. Our news articles, showcase shapers, professionals, and up and coming surf visionaries within the craft. Long wave magazine also offers resources for beginners, events to collaborate with more surfers, and an opportunity to connect and share your own story.

.

How it all began

Long Wave was created because of a curiousity and and desire to have a resource to inform those in the surf community of female surf accomploshments.

Looking to the future

We are looking to have more events and provide opportunites for and sponsor individuals to surf who do not have access to equipment or the sea.
